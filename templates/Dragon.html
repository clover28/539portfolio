The Dragon Scrolls

Summary

This is a mobile game that helps people learn Chinese characters. I worked with a partner on this project for EDUC 639 Design of Learning Environments in the Graduate School of Education, University of Pennsylvania.

My Role

I designed the gameplay related to the appearance of characters, the general structure of the game, and the interface. I also made a prototype of the gameplay for one level.

Tools

Sketch, Adobe Photoshop, InVision

View Prototype

I came up with idea of breaking down Chinese characters into puzzle pieces to learn the appearance of the characters, and paired up with my partner who was interested in using sound association between Chinese and English to learn the pronunciation of Chinese. The final product is a the combination of these two learning strategies in a fantasy world based on ancient Chinese myths.

Take aways

I was able to combine my knowledge in linguistics and passion for design. sharpened my design skills through this project and learned about the theories of educational games. I taught myself to use Sketch and InVision during this project. I also gained more experience about team work as I was constantly dicussing design ideas with my partner.
