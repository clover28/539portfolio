Mobile App: Beauty Organizer (Ongoing)

The Problem

How can we help people build a digital record of their makeup products and leverage this data?

This mobile app solves the problems caused by not being able to track one’s makeup products, such as buying duplicate products and not fully utilizing a product, and provides an enjoyable way to look at one’s makeup collection.

Competitive Research

The idea quickly expanded to a digital organizer for all kinds of makeup products. I did some interviews with my girl friends and confirmed that there is a huge need for it. In order to learn from existing solutions, I conducted a competitive analysis of four existing products, where I found out a list of pain points in current solutions.

  • Weaknesses in Current Solutions
    • Incomplete databases that miss many popular products
    • Database does not allow user input and is not up-to-date
    • For those that allow users to input product information, inputting process is very tedious
    • Database not searchable or inefficient search feature
    • Cannot sort or filter products in a user-friendly way
    • Boring visual style

From the competitive research, I decide to solve the problems of incomplete product database and tedious user input process. The app will allow user input to keep the database up-to-date, and the process of inputting product information will be automated and streamlined. It should provide sorting and filtering features that meet users' needs, and should also be pleasant to look at. In further market research and user research, I look to find out whether it should generalize suggestions on purchasing and using different products based on user data.

Personas

After interviewing potential users, I created three expert personas and one anti persona to better empathize with users and keep the product on the right direction.

Right now I am creating a journey map that describes the workflow of a typical user interacting with the app. The end product of this project will be a high-fidelity prototype. Stay tuned!

The Original Concept

This idea was initially just about lipsticks. It originated from the moment I was frustrated about figuring our what lipstick colors I own. What brand do I own most? What colors I have not had? Which of my lipsticks I have not made good use of and should start using them? How to coordinate lip colors with the rest of my look? I have many questions, and I do not want to answer them with an Excel spreadsheet. Here is a more glamorous way to organize lipsticks.
