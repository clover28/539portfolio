Mobile App: Beauty Keep

Overview

This mobile app allows users to track their makeup products and quickly find the products that are suitable for them. It solves the problems caused by not being able to track one’s makeup products, such as buying duplicate products and not fully utilizing a product, and saves the time spent on hunting for the perfect product.

Defining the problem

Competitive Analysis and User Interviews

I saw the problem of not being able to track the products I own, and conducted a competitive analysis of four existing products. I highlighted the problems in current solutions. I conducted 4 user interviews and created personas to give me a clear idea of users' needs.


  • Pain Points in Current Solutions
    • Incomplete databases that miss many popular products
    • Database does not allow user input and is not up-to-date
    • For those that allow users to input product information, inputting process is very tedious
    • Database not searchable or inefficient search feature
    • Cannot sort or filter products in a user-friendly way

By interviewing users who have different knowledge about products, I found that users want to:

    • A quick, easy way to input product information
    • Get meaningfu data from their collection
    • Powerful search, sort, and filter feature for products
    • Share their thoughts about products and makeup looks
    • Get recommendations on what to purchase and how to use products

Solution

Scan Products to Add

Image recognition allows users to add products in a snap. Users scan labels or the product itself.

View collection in a customizable the way

When viwing their own collection, users can view the summary cards at the top. They can also customize the order and content of these cards. They can search, sort, and filter products to quickly access the products they want to view.

Share makeup looks and get inspired

Users can share photos of their makeup looks and link the products used. They will be able to see related looks for each product and get a vivid idea of the effect of the products.

Intelligent notifications and recommendations

The friendly chatbot will notify users when they need to replace expired products, when they have not used some products in a while, or give users recommendations based on their preferences. The bot saves users time spent on researching products and analyzing their collection.
