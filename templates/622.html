Gale Researcher Needs Assessment and Usability Evaluation

Our team are conducting user research and usability tests for Gale Researcher, a web-based tool that helps undergraduate students with academic research. We look to help improve the confusing loop structure in the current workflow.


My Role

I am graphic designer on the team and also participate in every stage of user research project, from interviews to report writing.


Tools

Interviewing, Interaction Maps, Survey Design, Personas, Usability Testing; Adobe Illustrator

Interaction Map

After interviewing our client contact, we dived into the product and generated an interaction map that covers every action a user can take in the product. I led the discussion of the interaction map and drew on the whiteboard. We first figured out the workflow and then designed the layout for the map with post-it notes representing screenshots.
